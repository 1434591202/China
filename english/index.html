In the past 70 years, we have made great progress in improving people's livelihood.Our party always regards serving the people wholeheartedly as its fundamental purpose, and insists that the people share in the fruits of economic development and social progress.After 70 years of unremitting efforts, the people are now living a moderately prosperous life in all respects, from having enough food and clothing to overall well-off life.In 1978, the number of rural poor reached 770 million. By 2018, the number had dropped to 16.6 million.In particular, since the 18th national congress of the communist party of China (CPC), targeted poverty alleviation and targeted poverty alleviation have achieved unprecedented results.China has also built the world's largest social security system with the largest population.By June 2018, 925 million people had participated in basic old-age insurance, 191 million in unemployment insurance, and 230 million in work-related injury insurance.Today, the people are living a more prosperous life than ever before.
Seventy years, our mother country is getting better and better, stronger and stronger.Looking back over the past 70 years, our forefathers have taken every step with difficulty and sacrificed so much.When new China was founded, its economic strength was relatively backward, and the weapons on the parade ground were not enough to deter other countries.On the battlefield, great sacrifice for victory;The humiliation and outrage of the bombing of the embassy in Yugoslavia;Forget the south China sea collision incident helpless......But we came together, we stood up for sacrifice, and we survived every step of the way.
70 years, enough to let the hungry baby grow into a pillar of the country;Seventy years is enough for the running rivers to break through the vast plains;Seventy years is enough for the people to live a happy and healthy life and for the republic to regain its former glory.This year marks the 70th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, and 2021 marks the 100th anniversary of the founding of the communist party of China.China is closer to, more confident and capable of realizing the strategic goal of the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation than at any time in its history.The Chinese nation has made a great leap forward from standing up and becoming rich to becoming strong.
