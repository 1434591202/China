  • Born on November 12, 1910 in jintan district, changzhou, jiangsu province, he loved to use his brain when he was a child, and was often called "luo nerd" by his peers because he thought too intently.

  • In 1922, he graduated from renqu primary school at the age of 12 and entered jitan county junior high school. Mr. Wang weike found his talent in mathematics and tried his best to cultivate it.

  • In 1925, after graduating from junior high school, I studied in Shanghai zhonghua vocational school. Because I could not afford the tuition fee, I dropped out and went back home to help my father manage the grocery store. Therefore, I only got the junior high school diploma in my life.After that, he taught himself all the math courses in high school and junior college in five years.

  • In the autumn of 1927, he married wu xiaoyuan.

  • In the winter of 1929, he fell ill with typhoid fever, leaving his left leg permanently disabled and walking with the aid of a cane.

  • In 1929, hua luogeng was employed as a common servant in jintan middle school and began to publish papers in Shanghai magazines such as science.

  • 1931, served as an assistant in the department of mathematics, tsinghua university.He taught himself English, French, German and Japanese, and published three papers in foreign magazines.

  • In 1933, he was promoted to assistant professor.

  • In September 1934, he was promoted to lecturer.

  • In 1936, hua luogeng went to Cambridge university in England and spent two crucial years.By this time he had achieved many results in Waring's problem and benefited from the influence of the English hardy - littlewood school.At least 15 of his articles were published during his time at Cambridge.One of his papers about gauss earned him worldwide fame

  • In 1937, he returned to tsinghua university as a full professor, and later moved to national southwestern union university in kunming until 1945.

  • From 1939 to 1941, on a stilted building in kunming, I wrote more than 20 papers and completed the first mathematical monograph "theory of stacking prime Numbers".

  • From February to May 1946, he was invited to visit the Soviet union.In September of the same year, he visited the institute for advanced study in Princeton, USA.

  • In 1947, the theory of piling prime Numbers was published in the Russian version in the Soviet union.

  • In 1948, he was employed as a full professor at Illinois university until 1950.

  • Shortly after the founding of new China, hua luogeng resolutely decided to give up the favorable treatment in the United States, ran to the embrace of the motherland.

  • In the spring of 1950, he arrived in Beijing with his wife and children from the United States via Hong Kong and returned to qinghua garden, where he served as the director of the department of mathematics of tsinghua university.

  • In July 1952, invited by guo moruo, President of the Chinese academy of sciences, the institute of mathematics was established and served as its director.Joined zanu-pf in September.

  • In 1953, he joined a delegation of Chinese scientists to visit the Soviet union.He also attended the first world congress of mathematicians held in Hungary after world war ii, as well as the asia-pacific peace conference and the world peace council.

  • In 1955, he was elected member of the Chinese academy of sciences (academician).

  • In 1956, he set out to establish the institute of computational mathematics of the Chinese academy of sciences. His paper "on the function of multiple complex variables in typical domains" won the first prize of national natural science in 1956, and published monographs in Chinese, Russian and English.

  • In 1958, he served as vice President and head of the mathematics department of the university of science and technology of China, and applied to join the communist party of China in the same year.That same year, he and guo moruo led a Chinese delegation to a meeting in new Delhi on "coordination on science, technology and engineering issues."

  • After the beginning of the cultural revolution, is in the field to promote "double law" hua luogeng was rebel urgent call back to Beijing to write inspection, accept criticism.With his personal reputation, hua luogeng sent competent personnel to various places to set up the "promotion of excellent selection law, planning law team", personally led the team to all parts of the country to promote the "double law", wherever he went, all set off scientific experiments and practice of mass activities, achieved great economic and social benefits.

  • In 1969, optimization was published and the manuscript was presented to the state department for BBB 0's 20th anniversary.

  • In April 1970, in accordance with the instructions of premier zhou, the state council invited the heads of the seven ministry of industry to listen to hua luogeng lecture on the excellent selection law and the overall planning law.

  • In 1974, jiang qing, deputy head of the "central cultural revolution leading group", publicly named him in the commentary meeting of legalist works, saying that his promotion of "double law" to more than 20 provinces and cities was "a tour of mountains and waters".

  • In August 1975, when promoting "double method" in the great hinggan mountains, came to Harbin from the cutting site of the great hinggan mountains.He was in a coma for six weeks and was critically ill.

  • After crushing the gang of four, he was appointed vice President of the Chinese academy of sciences.His works have been published for many years.

  • In May 1979, he visited Western Europe for seven months to introduce his mathematical research results to his international counterparts.

  • In 1979, he was elected vice-chairman of the central committee of the democratic league.

  • In 1979, he was elected vice-chairman of the central committee of the democratic alliance.

  • In November 1982, he suffered a second heart attack.

  • In October 1983, he was invited to the United States by California institute of technology as a one-year lecturing activity.During his stay in the United States, he attended the founding conference of the third world academy of sciences in rialit, Italy and was elected as an academician.

  • In April 1984, he attended a ceremony in Washington to be awarded a foreign academician by the national academy of sciences, becoming the first Chinese to receive this honor.

  • In April 1985, he was elected vice chairman of the sixth session of the CPPCC national committee.

  • On June 3, 1985, he visited Japan at the invitation of Japan Asian cultural exchange association.

  • He died at 10:9pm on June 12, 1985, at the lecture hall of the department of mathematics and physics of Tokyo university, giving a lecture entitled "theoretical mathematics and its application" to the Japanese mathematical community.
