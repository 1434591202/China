  • Qian xuesen was born in Shanghai in 1911, with ancestral home in Lin 'an, hangzhou city, zhejiang province.In September 1923, he entered the middle school affiliated to Beijing normal university.In 1929, he was admitted to the department of railway engineering, school of mechanical engineering, Shanghai jiaotong university, ministry of railways. In 1934, he graduated from national jiaotong university (now Shanghai jiaotong university and xi 'an jiaotong university).

  • In September 1935, he entered the department of aeronautics and astronautics, Massachusetts institute of technology. In September 1936, he received a master's degree in aeronautical engineering from Massachusetts institute of technology. After that, he transferred to the department of aeronautics, California institute of technology.

  • He received a master's degree in aeronautical engineering and a doctorate in aeronautics and mathematics.From July 1938 to August 1955, qian xuesen was engaged in the research of aerodynamics, solid mechanics, rockets, missiles and other fields in the United States. Together with his tutor, he completed the research project of high-speed aerodynamics and established the "kamen qian xuesen" formula

  • In 1939, he received his doctorate in aeronautics and mathematics from California institute of technology.In 1943, he became an assistant professor at the California institute of technology.In 1945, he became an associate professor at the California institute of technology.In 1947, he became a professor at the Massachusetts institute of technology

  • In 1947, she married jiang ying in Shanghai.In 1949, he was director and professor of the jet propulsion center at Caltech.In 1953, qian xuesen formally put forward the concept of physical mechanics, advocated to determine the macroscopic mechanical properties of matter from its microscopic laws, and opened up a new field of high temperature and high pressure.In 1954, engineering cybernetics was published in English, with Russian, German and Chinese versions published in 1956, 1957 and 1958 respectively.In 1958, he became dean of modern mechanics department of university of science and technology of China.

  • In 1949, the People's Republic of China announced the birth of the news spread to the United States, qian xuesen and his wife jiang ying will discuss an early return to the motherland, for their own country.At this time, the United States, led by McCarthy to carry out a comprehensive investigation of the communists, and set off a frenzy of driving employees to the United States government.Qian xuesen, suspected of being a communist and refusing to expose his friends, was suddenly stripped by the us military of his certificate for confidential research.Qian xuesen was very angry, as a request to return to the reason

  • In 1950, when Mr. Qian was on his way home from port, he was stopped by American officials and put in prison, and Dan a. Kimball, the vice admiral of the United States navy, claimed that he could go anywhere with the strength of five divisions.[5] from then on, qian xuesen was persecuted by the American government, and at the same time lost his precious freedom.Immigration agents raided his home and detained him on temina island for 14 days before releasing him on a hefty $15,000 bond from the California institute of technology.Later, customs confiscated his luggage, including 800 kilograms of books and notebooks.American prosecutors reviewed all his material again before proving his innocence.

  • News of qian xuesen's persecution in the United States in the early 1950s soon spread to China, and Chinese friends in the scientific and technological community supported qian through various channels.The CPC central committee is deeply concerned about qian xuesen's situation in the United States, and the Chinese government has issued a public statement condemning the U.S. government for imprisoning qian against its will

  • In 1954, by chance, he saw Chen shutong standing on the gate tower of tian 'anmen square in a newspaper, and he decided to write to his father's good friend for help.While premier zhou enlai was worried about this, Chen shutong, then vice chairman of the standing committee of the National People's Congress, received a letter from the other side of the ocean.He opened it and signed "qian xuesen", asking the Chinese government to help him return home.On June 5, 1954, secretary general of the Chinese delegation wang bingnan began preliminary discussions with the representative of the United States, deputy secretary of state Johnson, on the issue of expatriates between the two countries.The United States has presented China with a list of Chinese nationals and some American military personnel detained by China, asking for a chance to return home.In order to show China's sincerity, zhou enlai instructed wang bingnan to make generous concessions in the third china-us talks held on June 15, 1954, and at the same time demanded the United States to stop detaining qian xuesen and other Chinese nationals staying in the United States.However, China's legitimate request was unreasonably rejected by the us side.Geneva conference closed on 21 July 1954.In order not to interrupt communication channels, zhou enlai instructed wang bingnan and the United States to agree to hold consular level talks in Geneva from July 22, 1954.In order to further said Chinese on the sincerity of the u.s.-china talks held in China has released four American pilots, China made concessions, in the end is for qian xuesen to return home as soon as possible, the us scientists on this key question, however, the United States representative Johnson or to China to take out the real reason of qian xuesen to return home, don't let go.

  • In 1955, after premier zhou enlai's relentless efforts at diplomatic negotiations with the United States -- including the release of 11 American airmen captured during the Korean war in exchange -- qian xuesen received a notice from the United States immigration service to allow him to return home on August 4, 1955.On September 17, 1955, qian xuesen's wish to return to China finally came true. On this day, qian xuesen, his wife jiang ying and a pair of young children boarded the "President of Cleveland" ship and embarked on the journey back to his motherland.On October 8, 1955, qian xuesen and his family finally returned to their homeland

  • In early 1956, he submitted to the CPC central committee and the state council the opinion on the establishment of China's national defense aviation industry.In the same year, the state council and the central military commission established the aviation industry commission, the leading body for missile and aviation science research, and appointed him as its member.

  • In 1956 to participate in China's first five years the determination of scientific planning, qian xuesen and qian weichang, Qian Sanqiang, zhou enlai called China's "three money" technology, institute of qian xuesen to establish China's first rocket, missile - defense research institute and dean as the first 5, ren, director of the institute of mechanics, Chinese academy of sciences, researchers.He worked at the institute of mechanics until about 1972.At the second plenary session of the second national committee of the CPPCC, he was co-elected as a member of the second national committee of the CPPCC.

  • In 1957, on qian xuesen's initiative, the Chinese mechanics society was founded, and qian xuesen was unanimously elected as the first President.On February 18, premier zhou enlai signed an order appointing qian xuesen as the first President of the fifth research institute of the ministry of national defense.On November 16, premier zhou enlai appointed qian xuesen as President of the first branch of the fifth research institute of the ministry of national defense.In the same year, qian xuesen's engineering cybernetics won the first prize of the natural science award of the Chinese academy of sciences and was elected as a member of the academic department of the Chinese academy of sciences.In June 1957, the preparatory committee of Chinese association of automation was established in Beijing, with qian xuesen as the chairman.In September of the same year, the founding congress of the federation of international controls elected qian xuesen as the standing director of the first IFAC council.

  • In 1958, in order to train talents for the two bombs and one star project, the university of science and technology of China (ustc) was established in response to qian xuesen's request to establish an "interstellar astronautics academy". Qian xuesen was the dean of modern mechanics department of ustc and became one of the founders of ustc.Through the introduction of du runsheng and Yang gang yi, he joined the communist party of China.

  • In 1959, he was elected as a deputy to the second National People's Congress.He was elected to the third, fourth and fifth National People's Congress.

  • On September 19, 1959, qian xuesen made a special trip from Beijing to visit the campus of xi 'an jiaotong university, which had moved from Shanghai to xi 'an.

  • In 1960, he became the deputy director of the fifth research institute of the ministry of national defense, and no longer served as the President of a branch of the institute.Since then, qian xuesen's main duties have been deputy director of the fifth research institute, vice minister of the seventh machinery industry, and deputy director of the national defense science and technology commission.

  • On November 15, 1960, under the personal guidance of marshal nie rongzhen, the test committee with general zhang aiping as its director and sun jixian, qian xuesen and wang zheng as its deputy directors successfully organized the flight test of the first short-range missile made in China at the jiuquan launch site.

  • In 1961, he was elected President of the first council of China association of automation.1962 handouts on physical mechanics were published.1963 an introduction to interstellar navigation was published.In 1965, he became vice minister of the 7th ministry of mechanical industry (ministry of missile industry).

  • On October 27, 1966, qian xuesen assisted marshal nie rongzhen to directly lead the "two-bomb combination" flight experiment of delivering atomic bomb with medium-range missile at jiuquan launch site, which achieved complete success

  • In 1968, he was also the director of the fifth research institute of the people's liberation army (today's China academy of space technology)

  • In 1969, he was elected as a representative of the ninth national congress of the communist party of China and an alternate member of the ninth central committee.He was elected as a deputy to the 10th, 11th, 12th, 13th, 14th and 15th national congress and an alternate member of the 10th, 11th and 12th central committee.

  • In 1970, he became deputy director of the commission for science and technology for national defense and no longer served as President of the fifth research institute of the people's liberation army

  • In 1979, he won the Distinguished Alumni Award of the California institute of technology, the year China and the United States formally established diplomatic relations.But qian xuesen did not travel to the United States to receive the honor.

  • In 1979, he won the Distinguished Alumni Award of the California institute of technology, the year China and the United States formally established diplomatic relations.But qian xuesen did not travel to the United States to receive the honor.

  • In 1980, he was elected vice chairman of the first national committee of the association of science and technology of China.In 1986, he was elected chairman of the third national committee of the association of science and technology of China.

  • In 1982, he became deputy director of the science and technology commission of the commission of science, technology and industry for national defense.Elected honorary President of the Chinese society of mechanics.He is the director of the fifth research institute of the ministry of national defense and also the director of one branch of the institute (today's China academy of launch vehicle technology)."On systems engineering" was published. 1988 "on systems engineering" (revised edition) was published.

  • In 1984, he was elected as the executive chairman of the presidium of the Chinese academy of sciences at the fifth conference of academic members (academicians) of the Chinese academy of sciences.

  • In 1985, qian xuesen, as the first prize winner and tu shoue, yao tongbin, hao fujian, liang sili, zhuang fenggan and li xune, won the special prize of national scientific and technological progress for his contribution to China's strategic missile technology.

  • In 1986, at the fourth plenary session of the sixth national committee of the CPPCC, he was co-elected vice chairman of the sixth national committee of the CPPCC, and successively elected vice chairman of the seventh and eighth national committee of the CPPCC.

  • In 1987, he was employed as senior adviser of the science and technology commission of the national defense science, technology and industry commission.Science and systematic engineering of socialist modernization was published.On May 3, 1987, he became honorary President of Chinese society of human sciences.

  • In 1988, he was also the director of the science and technology committee of the seventh national committee of the CPPCC.Won the special prize of national science and technology progress award in 1985.On the science of the mind was published.

  • In 1989, he was awarded "W" by the international conference on technology and technology exchange and the international institute of science and technology.F.The rockwell junior medal is awarded as "world-class scientific and engineering celebrity" and "honorary member of the international institute of science and technology".Qian xuesen anthology (1938 ~ 1956) was published.

  • In 1991, at the first plenary session of the fourth national committee of the association of science and technology of China, he was awarded the title of honorary chairman of the association.He was elected honorary President of the China institute of aerodynamics (renamed China aerodynamics society in 1989).Elected honorary President of Chinese society of systems engineering.

  • In 1994, he was elected as an academician of Chinese academy of engineering at the first academician conference of Chinese academy of engineering.On geographic science was published.Urban studies and landscape cities was published.

  • In 1995, he was awarded the first ho leung ho lee foundation excellence award (1994) (later renamed the ho leung ho lee foundation science and technology achievement award).

  • In 1995, with the approval of the propaganda department of the CPC central committee, xi 'an jiaotong university library was named qian xuesen library.

  • In 1998, he was employed as a senior adviser to the science and technology committee of the PLA general armament department.He was awarded the title of "senior academician of Chinese academy of sciences" and "senior academician of Chinese academy of engineering" at the 9th academician conference of Chinese academy of sciences and the 4th academician conference of Chinese academy of engineering.

  • In 1999, he was awarded the "two bombs and one star medal" by the CPC central committee, the state council and the central military commission.

  • On the occasion of his 90th birthday on December 11, 2001, qian xuesen wrote an inscription for his Alma mater Shanghai jiao tong university: "we hope that all the teachers and students of Shanghai jiao tong university will inherit and carry forward the fine traditions of his Alma mater, love the motherland, advocate science, pursue truth and serve the people, and strive to build Shanghai jiao tong university into a world-class university in the 21st century.Qian xuesen has always been concerned about his Alma mater and fully developed the glorious tradition of thinking about the source of drinking water in jiaotong university.

  • Qian xuesen passed away at 8:6am Beijing time on October 31, 2009. He was 98 years old
