The development course

In 2006, 38 research institutes entered the field of DSP chip design for the first time. It took 6 years to launch the first practical high performance floating point general DSP in China, which was independent from instruction set, architecture and development environment

Since 2012, the scientific research team again after six years of continuous improvement and modification, through the actual use of the process of constant iteration and run-in, completed the "soul core" family of the s.

On April 23, 2018, the first digital China construction summit hosted by ccec 38 released the digital signal processor with the highest practical computing performance in the industry -- "soul core ii A".

"Soul core ii A" means what?

"China chip" is capable of 100 billion operations per second.

  • A chip just a little bigger than a dollar coin, yet capable of billions of floating point operations per second...The exciting results were released on April 23 by the China institute of electrical engineering.After six years of hard work, the scientists of 38 institute have made the most powerful single-core digital signal chip in the industry, realizing the "core" and "soul" of the Chinese nation, making the "intelligent brain" of all kinds of equipment like a tiger with wings.Our reporter to China electricity division chief scientist, "soul core 2 A" chief designer hong yi conducted an exclusive interview.

  • "In the future, from the perspective of technology, we should build a software-based processing platform. First, we should try to make products pedigree and series.Secondly, the application field should be refined to make "soul core" series chips more efficient and competitive, providing strong support for artificial intelligence computing and big data computing.On the morning of April 24, in the office of 38 institute of electric power in China, chief scientist of Chinese electric power, "soul core 2 A" chief designer hong yi accepted the exclusive interview of our reporter.

  • "Compared with 'soul core I', 'soul core ii A' performance and universality is better, now has the capacity of mass production."Hong, according to A "number one" soul core is the first in our country from the instruction set, system structure, development environment are independent design of DSP chip, "no. 2, A" soul core adopts the same autonomous system architecture, appearance is A little bigger than the one yuan coin, but its operation performance is 4.8 times that of "number one" soul core and interface more and better use, has relatively good application environment and the debugging method.For example, "soul core I" was used at that time is A single-core processor, 68 computing components, more than 600 instructions, 24Mb storage capacity, and "soul core ii A" in these aspects has been fully upgraded, single core into A dual-core, computing components expanded to 84, instructions increased to more than 1400, storage capacity expanded to 48Mb.
