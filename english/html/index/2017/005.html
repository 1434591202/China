  • Type 055 destroyers, load displacement is more than 14000 tons of the ship, more than South Korea's "king sejong" class, equipped with a new generation of 112 unit of general vertical launch device, can accommodate a maximum of 448 kinds of missiles, large type 055 missile destroyer also realize the 3 sets of front, four bands of powerful detection capability, has "x + s" dual-band active phased array radar.

  • The main antenna of the type 055 guided-missile destroyer is designed in conformation, which has a high level of information and stealth performance, and can organize three layers of early warning defense network, such as far, middle and near, and has strong capability of air defense, anti-missile, anti-submarine, anti-ship, land attack and electronic warfare.The ship has high endurance, self-support and seaworthiness, and can carry out combat missions in the unlimited navigation area except polar region.The first vessel of this class was launched at Shanghai jiangnan shipyard on June 28, 2017.

  • For the Chinese navy, the type 055 destroyer not only utilizes many new technologies and design concepts, but also is the first ship of the Chinese navy to reach the world's advanced or even partial leading level in platform and design concept upon service, which is even more significant than the first home-made aircraft carrier just launched by China.

  • This class of ship is also the first time to realize the great potential excellent platform of long-range attack and defense in the design of the drive and protection ship. The improved model further adds electromagnetic propulsion, electromagnetic gun, laser close-in defense technology and sea-based anti-missile capability, which can guarantee China's national security and overseas interests.
