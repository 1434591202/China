Mozi quantum science experiment satellite

reason name

The first quantum communication satellite was named after mozi, an ancient Chinese scientist.Mozi first proposed the idea that light travels in a straight line.

Mozi was the first to discover the straight-line propagation of light through the pinhole imaging experiment, which laid a certain foundation for the development of quantum communication.Mozi also proposed particle theory in a sense.The experimental satellite, named after the Chinese scientist mozi, reflects China's cultural confidence.

What does "mozi" mean?

China has realized the quantum communication between satellite and ground for the first time in the world.

 back

  • Quantum satellite was approved in December 2011, and is one of the first batch of space science pilot satellites of Chinese academy of sciences.In addition, the project has also built ground science application systems including four ground stations of quantum communication, nanshan, delingha, xinglong and lijiang, and ali quantum teleportation experimental stations, which together with quantum satellites constitute an integrated quantum science experiment system of heaven and earth.

  • The advent of quantum communication system has ignited the hope of building "absolutely safe" communication system.At present, the application and industrialization of quantum communication has become the goal that every big country is scrambling for.In the international race of quantum communication, China is a latecomer.After years of efforts, China has become one of the world's first-class quantum information research ranks, in the metropolitan quantum communication technology is also at the forefront of the world, the construction of hefei, jinan and other large-scale quantum communication man, "beijing-shanghai trunk line" large-scale optical fiber quantum communication backbone network is also about to be completed.

  • Quantum satellite is one of the first batch of space science pilot special scientific experimental satellites of Chinese academy of sciences. Its main scientific objectives are firstly to conduct satellite and ground high-speed quantum key distribution experiment with the help of satellite platform, and conduct wide-area quantum key network experiment on this basis, so as to make a major breakthrough in the practical application of space quantum communication.The second is to conduct experiments on quantum entanglement distribution and quantum teleportation at space scale to test the completeness of quantum mechanics at space scale.

  • In the next two years, micius will carry out four experimental missions in the world for the first time to achieve two major scientific goals: to conduct "satellite-ground high-speed quantum key distribution experiment" via satellite relay, and to conduct "wide-area quantum communication network experiment" on this basis, in order to make a major breakthrough in the practical use of quantum communication in space;(third objective) to conduct experimental research on the completeness test of space-scale quantum mechanics, including "star-ground two-way entanglement distribution experiment" and "space-scale quantum teleportation experiment".In addition to the satellite, four quantum communication ground stations (located in xinglong, hebei province, urumqi, xinjiang province, delingha, qinghai province and lijiang, yunnan province) and the quantum teleportation experimental station in ali, Tibet have been built on the ground.In addition, scientists from the Austrian academy of sciences and the university of Vienna have set up ground stations in Vienna and graz in cooperation with the Chinese.
