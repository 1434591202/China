Liquid metal machine

The product features

The liquid metal machine has an unusual set of behaviors that are quite similar to those of some simple mollusks in nature. For example, it can "eat" food (fuel), move autonomously, can be deformed, and has certain metabolic functions (chemical reactions), so the authors named it liquid metal mollusk.

The liquid metal can swallow small amounts of substances in deformable machine form after long time high speed movement, achieved without external power of voluntary movement, to develop practical intelligent motor, blood vessels, robot, fluid pumping system, flexible actuator and even more complex liquid metal robots laid the foundation theory and technology.

What does "liquid metal machine" mean?

Completely out of the external power system of confused, in the base liquid gallium alloy electrolyte can be through the "intake" aluminum as food or fuel to provide energy, realize high speed.

  • Among all kinds of flexible machines developed so far, the deformation capacity, running speed and life level of autonomous liquid metal machines are rare, which adds many important USES to them.As one of the specific application devices, the paper also shows the first liquid metal pump without external power. By restricting it to the valve seat, it can achieve the purpose of self-rotating and pumping the fluid. Based on this, a large number of micropumps can be rapidly produced to meet the transportation requirements of liquid medicine and array microfluid, with very low cost.If this kind of flexible pump is used for cooling, a microchip cooler with high integration can be realized.Further applications could lead to blood vessel or cavity robots or even self-assembling liquid metal intelligent machines.

  • The dynamic mechanism of this autonomous liquid metal machine comes from two aspects: firstly, Galvanic cell effect occurring among liquid alloy, metal fuel and electrolyte generates endogenous electric field, which induces asymmetric response of high surface tension on liquid metal surface, and then generates strong thrust on liquid metal machine prone to deformation.At the same time, the hydrogen produced by the electrochemical reaction gives a further boost.It is this dual action that leads to the extraordinary behavior of liquid metal motors, and this energy conversion mechanism has important implications for the development of special forms of energy power systems.

  • The emergence of self-driven liquid metal machine leads to a new concept of deformable machine, which will significantly speed up the development process of flexible intelligent machine.At present, the research and development activities around the world are in full swing. If liquid metal can fully display the huge potential, combined with related technologies, it will trigger many changes beyond the traditional machine.Liu jing's group's discovery of liquid metal self-drive effect and corresponding machine morphology opens up a new way for the future development of advanced flexible intelligent robot technology, which has very important scientific significance and practical application value.
