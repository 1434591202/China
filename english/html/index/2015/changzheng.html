The long March 6

Get the honor

On November 3, 2015, the long March 6 carrier rocket won the highest honor of the 17th China international industrial exposition, a special honor award.

What does the long March 6 mean?

It marks the addition of a new member of China's long march carrier rocket family, and sets a new record for China's space launch of multiple satellites with one rocket.

 back

  • Cost reduction is an important condition to improve the competitive ability of launch vehicle in satellite launch market at home and abroad.On the one hand, make full use of the basic technology of the new-generation carrier rocket to reduce the development cost.Simplify launch operations and reduce launch costs.With kerosene/liquid oxygen engines, fuel costs are millions of dollars lower than before.

  • High reliability is the lifeblood of a rocket.Follow the reliability design principle of "simple is reliable", strive to be simple in single machine or system design;Through strict quality assurance system and quality management system, control product design and production quality.The reliability of the design is 95 percent, and after further optimization, the reliability of the first flight has been improved to 98 percent, which is 1 percent higher than the current safest long march ii F model.

  • To meet the various needs of users, it can not only launch small satellites in various orbits, but also launch double or multiple satellites.It can accommodate both new launch sites and launch from existing sites.The long March 6 has set a new record for a Chinese satellite launch with 20 satellites in one rocket.

  • The basic technologies of the new-generation carrier rocket should be fully applied, with a short development period, simplified launch mode and shortened launch period, so as to meet the requirements of rapid launch.Launch preparation for the first flight takes only a week.

  • Designers of the long March 6 rocket specifically developed iterative guidance technology.With the new technology, if the rocket deviates from its orbit during flight, it does not need to return to the predetermined orbit, but directly plans the optimal route from its position to the final orbit.
