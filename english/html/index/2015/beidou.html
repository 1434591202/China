BDS

development history

In 2009, the construction of the beidou-3 system was launched.

In 2003, the third geostationary satellite was launched to further enhance the system performance.

In 2004, the construction of the beidou-2 system was launched.

By the end of 2012, 14 satellites had been launched into space.

By the end of 2018, China will have completed the launching and networking of 19 satellites, completed the construction of basic systems, and provided global services.

What does beidou satellite navigation system mean?

It can provide all kinds of users with high precision and reliable positioning, navigation and timing services, and short message communication capability.
