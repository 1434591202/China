Pearl river delta bridge

The development course

Construction of the hong kong-zhuhai-macao bridge began on December 15, 2009

The main project was completed on July 7, 2017.

The main project acceptance was completed on February 6, 2018.

It began operation at 9:00 am on October 24, 2018.

What does the bridge mean?

Greatly shortening the time and space distance between Hong Kong, zhuhai and Macao;

  • The hong kong-zhuhai-macao bridge is a national project and an important part of the country. The construction of the bridge is among the world's most remarkable. It embodies the spirit of a country's struggle to bridge mountains and rivers, China's comprehensive national strength and independent innovation capacity, and its national aspiration to forge ahead in the world.This is a dream bridge, concentric bridge, self-confidence bridge, Renaissance bridge.The completion of the bridge and its opening to traffic have further strengthened our confidence in the path, theory, system and culture of socialism with Chinese characteristics.
