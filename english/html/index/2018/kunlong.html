Kun dragon - 600

The development course

At 9:05 am on October 20, 2018, a domestically made large amphibious aircraft, the kunlong AG600, successfully took off and landed at zhanghe airport in jingmen, hubei province.

On October 25, 2018, China's first large amphibious aircraft, the "kun dragon" AG600, successfully completed its first flight on water, returned to zhuhai from zhanghe airport in jingmen, hubei province.

On December 20, 2018, "kun dragon" was chosen as the top ten buzzwords of 2018 for science and technology。

What does kun lung -600 mean?

One of China's three big aircraft musketeers

 back

  • Based on the water-h-5, the kun dragon 600 is a comprehensive rescue aircraft similar in size to the A320, with a maximum take-off weight of 53.5 tons and a maximum range of 4,500 kilometers.The world's largest amphibious aircraft, can land and land on land and water, using a three-man crew, two pilots, a mechanic.

  • Kun dragon 600 aircraft, using large length-width ratio hull type, cantilever trapezoid on a single wing;"T" tail fin, front three point retractable landing gear layout, using integrated avionics system, equipped with infrared detection and optical photography and other search and detection equipment;Emergency rescue facilities for the provision of water pumping systems and water rescue mainly include berths for critically ill and sick persons, rescue boats, rescue suits, stretchers, simple emergency operating facilities and medicines, etc.

  • The kun dragon 600 is capable of quickly and efficiently extinguishing forest fires and providing timely and effective sea rescue.The kun lung 600 will be able to draw 12 tonnes of water at a time for no more than 20 seconds. It will be able to anchor at the surface of the water for rescue operations. It will be able to rescue up to 50 people in distress at a time.

  • According to the design idea of "one aircraft, multiple types, amphibious and series development", the "kun long-600" aircraft can be designed with necessary equipment and facilities according to the needs of users, so as to meet the needs of Marine environmental monitoring, resource detection, passenger and cargo transport and other tasks.

  • Using its characteristics of relatively large payload, slow flight speed and low flight height, the kun dragon 600 is able to 'make full use of its advantages and avoid disadvantages' when fighting high-rise buildings and forest fires.In addition, it can take off and fall on the water surface, draw water quickly and throw water accurately, which can put out fire in high-rise buildings and forests in time.The aircraft can go back and forth between the water source and the fire site for many times, throwing water to put out the fire, which can be called the most professional "fire-fighting machine".
