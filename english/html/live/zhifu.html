It centers on mobile terminals to settle and pay the products purchased through mobile terminals. The main form of mobile payment is mobile payment.

Mobile payment is a derivative of third-party payment.The so-called third-party payment refers to that in the transactions through the third-party payment platform, the buyer purchases commodities and then USES the account provided by the third-party platform to make payment for goods, and the third-party notifies the seller that the payment arrives and makes delivery.Upon inspection, the buyer notifies the seller to pay, and a third party transfers the payment to the seller's account.Some researchers believe that the third-party payment, as a credit intermediary in essence, provides certain credit guarantee for the payment activities of transactions, so as to eliminate the credit risk problems caused by asymmetric information between buyers and sellers.
