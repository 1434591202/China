China high-speed railway (HSR) refers to the passenger dedicated railway lines (PDL) that are newly designed to run trains of 250 km/h (including reserved trains) or above with the initial operating speed of no less than 200 km/h.In the broad sense of network construction, high-speed railway in China refers to all new railway lines within China with a design speed of 200km/h or above and some existing railway lines that have been upgraded to the standard.
