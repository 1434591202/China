high speed railway Leading global

China's high-speed rail system is developing rapidly. A "four vertical and four horizontal" network has been built, and the "eight vertical and eight horizontal" plan has been gradually improved.From scratch, from weak to strong, from following to leading, China's high-speed railway has cast a new "national card" in just ten years.

5G era One hundred times more efficiency

5G is characterized by ultra-high speed, super-large connection and ultra-low time delay, and the communication rate will be 10-100 times higher than 4G. Cloud computing, AI, unmanned aerial vehicles, VR and big video in the 5G ecosystem will develop simultaneously.

mobile payment convenient and swift

A "cashless society" seems to be coming soon in China, where you can meet your daily needs even if you are "penniless" and have only a mobile phone.The rapid development of China's mobile payment market makes people in particular sigh, it can be seen that mobile payment has been everywhere in everyone's life.

artificial intelligence barrier-free communication

For a computer, it is difficult to understand language, but relatively easy to understand text.Today, when artificial intelligence develops to a mature stage, this barrier no longer exists. For example, the SIRI function of the current apple phone can understand human speech, and we can communicate with it without any obstacles.
