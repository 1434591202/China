During the 70 years since its founding, countless heroes have dedicated themselves to it.They succeeded. They became famous.But who knows how many failures they have experienced behind their success and how much selflessness there is in their dedication?

salute 

I have dedicated my life to the cause of our country, stand up the backbone of China's rise, and pay tribute to them together!

In today's world, where material desires are booming, it is hard to believe that anyone would devote his life to something. By dedication, I mean pure dedication without any impurities. This dedication is not for fame, wealth or status, and it is not expected to be immortified by it.When such a person exists, we must call him a hero, not only for his greatness, but also for his rarity.In most professions, at least, such people are rare.

Qian Xuesen

World-renowned scientist, aerodynamicist, founder of China's manned space flight, academician of Chinese academy of sciences and Chinese academy of engineering, winner of China's two-bomb and one-star merit medal, known as "the father of China's space", "the father of China's missile", "the father of China's automatic control" and "the king of rockets".

Deng Jiaxian

Deng jiaxian, the main organizer and leader of China's nuclear weapons research and development, has always been at the forefront of China's weapons manufacturing, leading many scholars and technicians, and successfully designed China's atomic bomb and hydrogen bomb, leading China's defense self-defense weapons to the world's advanced level.

Loo-Keng Hua

He is the founder and pioneer of Chinese analytic number theory, matrix geometry, canonical group, self-preserving function theory and multiple complex function theory, and is listed as one of the 88 great mathematical figures in the world in Chicago science and technology museum.International mathematical research achievements named after him include "Fahrenheit theorem", "Fahrenheit inequality", "hua-wang method" and so on.
