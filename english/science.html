Skill in China

The scientific and technological level of the People's Republic of China has been greatly improved, and major scientific and technological achievements have been made in the fields of computer, aerospace, biological engineering, new energy, new materials and laser technology.In the 1980s and 1990s, the Chinese government launched the "863 plan" and the "strategy of rejuvenating the country through science and education", which greatly promoted the development and progress of science and technology in the country.
China has numerous public research institutions, including the Chinese academy of sciences, the Chinese academy of engineering and many research universities.There is a large amount of state funding for scientific research every year.International competitiveness in vlsi, supercomputers, aerospace, controllable thermonuclear fusion and other fields is also rapidly improving.

  • 1949

    On November 1, 1949, the Chinese academy of sciences (cas) was founded in Beijing.The first President guo moruo, vice presidents li siguang, tao menghe, zhu kezhen, Chen boda, etc.Under the academy of sciences, the planning bureau, compilation and translation bureau, liaison bureau and other units are mainly engaged in the research of major comprehensive subjects in natural sciences, philosophy, basic sciences of social sciences, emerging technologies, national economy and national defense construction.

  • 1958

    China's first research heavy water reactor with a power of 7,000 kw and a cyclotron with a power of 2 mev were built and officially operated in the tuoli district of fangshan.People's Daily published the news on July 1st.A grand handover ceremony was held at the engineering site on September 27.Party and state leaders Chen yi and nie rongzhen attended the ceremony.The ceremony was presided over by zhang jinfu.Nie rongzhen, director of the national acceptance committee, signed for acceptance.

  • 1964

    At 3 PM on October 16, 1964, China successfully exploded its first atomic bomb in the western region, becoming the fifth country in the world with nuclear weapons after the United States, the Soviet union, Britain and France.Declare that we will no longer be bullied.

  • 1970

    Dongfanghong-1, the first man-made earth satellite launched by China, was developed by the China academy of space technology with qian xuesen as the first President and launched at 21:35 hours on April 24, 1970.The successful launch of the satellite marks the fifth country in the world to launch its own satellite on a home-made rocket, after the Soviet union, the United States, France and Japan.

  • 1973

    In 1973, yuan longping developed indica hybrid rice for the first time in the world, and after that, he succeeded in cultivating "three-line hybrid rice", and now the farmland in the north and south of China is widely cultivated.The large-scale application of hybrid rice has played an important role in increasing grain yield in China.More than 20 countries and regions, including India and Vietnam, have also introduced hybrid rice.
